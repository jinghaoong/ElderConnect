ElderConnect - Caretakers' Assistant

A feature rich application to better manage patients' needs.

Motivation

Ageing Population

Is an issue that Singapore is starting to face and will continue to face in the future. One of the concerns of the elderly’s working children is that there will be no one taking care of their parents at home, and hiring a domestic worker may not fit their budget or there may be worries about elderly abuse.
